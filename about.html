ABOUT US
     

Movie Mania is an online movie ticket booking website where the user can book tickets, watch trailers of upcoming and latest movies,songs and mant more.

Movie Mania offers a grand collection of cinema formats.Movie Mania is actually a (group)project made by Diploma students of Computer Department(3 rd Year) of M.H Saboo Siddik Polytechnic located in Byculla. It was fun making this website. It has home page,feedbackpage,login page,sign on page,ticket page,about page,etc.We used many languages like HTML,CSS,Javascript for developing this website.

Here,are the names of the students who developed this website 1.Ansari Mehjabin,Ansari Raheen and Khwaja Arshi. We added many pictures,movies in our wesite. User can view trailers of movies including hollywood,bollywood,telugu movies,marathi movies ,etc.User can also signin and login from here.One can also book tickets for desired movies.
